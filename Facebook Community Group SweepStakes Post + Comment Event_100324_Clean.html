  2. Each entrant who meets eligibility requirements will be provided one entry maximum per person (collectively, an “Entry Requirement”) with each entry eligible to win one prize only. Entrants must complete the Entry Requirement in its entirety in order to receive one (1) entry.
  3. All eligible entries will be collected and grouped together for a random selection via online generator of ten (10) winners. Winners will be selected at random on October 15, 2024 at approximately 2:00pm PDT at Sponsor’s office. Once a winner is selected, they are no longer eligible for any further winnings as each entrant is allowed one (1) entry maximum, and if further disqualified from a second Prize.
  4. FASHIONGO will announce that winners have been selected through a post in the FASHIONGO Community Facebook group (“FB Group”) on October 17, 2024 at approximately 2:00pm PDT. Entrants must check their Facebook Messenger (or email for mail in entries) for a notification regarding their individual win, as winners will be directly contacted by FASHIONGO via Facebook Messenger for the email address used to register their FASHIONGO buyer account. Once confirmed, FASHIONGO will send a promo code offering a $100 discount for a $200 minimum order placed on www.fashiongo.net (“Promo Code”) to each verified winner directly via Facebook Messenger, unless otherwise agreed upon in writing by Sponsor and winner, which will be sent out no later than October 22, 2024 at 11:59pm PDT.  It is winner’s sole responsibility to provide correct email address to Sponsor. 
  5. Total value of all ten (10) prizes is valued at $1000.  There will be ten (10) winners selected.  Each of the ten winners will receive one (1) Promo Code (each a “Prize” and collectively “Prizes”).  If there are fewer than 10 entries for the Sweepstake, all entries that meet eligibility requirements will win one Prize, and the remaining Prizes, if any, will not be disbursed.
  6. List of winners will be provided upon written request to Sponsor, at sponsors address as stated below.
